JPG

  • Good for storing photos, not art- not great color quality
  • Lower quality than PNG
  • Used on the internet frequently because they can be compressed so well
  • However, image quality degreades when compressed

GIF

  • Very limited color range
  • Much lower quality, very pixelated
  • Used frequently on the internet
  • Not great for photos or storage

PNG

  • Supports transparency
  • Great for internet
  • Compresses without compromising quality
  • Larger, good for large images
  • Good color quality
