Harbin's Attractions

Harbin Snow and Ice Festival

Harbin's main attraction is its Harbin Snow and Ice festival, located at the magnificent Harbin Snow and Ice World. During this festival, entire buildings are formed out of ice. This is the premier tourist attraction in Harbin. Pictured on the right is a huge ice scupture, like those found at the festival.

Historic Streets

Harbin is great city to walk around in. It has old cobblestone streets, which are free of cars and bicycles. One great street to visit is the famous "Zhongyang Dajie."

Shopping

Harbin is a major trade and shopping center in the region. Home to designer strores such as Versace, Calvin Klein, Gucci,and Louis Vuitton, this city is a great place to shop for high-end clothing and accessories.
