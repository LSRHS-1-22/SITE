Come to Harbin, China!

Harbin, China is a city like no other. It is known as "The Ice City" because of its harsh winters. Located in northeastern China, these winters yield the beauty of the acclaimed Harbin International Ice and Snow Scuplture Festival, pictured on the right. Harbin was occupied by Russia for many years, so it is full of Russian culture and architecture.
