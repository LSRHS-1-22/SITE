Shangri-la Hotel Harbin

This 5 star hotel is near many of the attractions located in Harbin. Rooms start at $128 a night, but the price is well worth the magnificant decor, as well as the view it offers.


Kempinsky Hotel Harbin

This upscale hotel contains a beatiful pool and spa area, and good restaurants. With rooms starting at $91, this hotel is a great choice.


Wanda Realm Harbin

This grand hotel contains a great pool, as well as an unbelievable interior. Rooms start at $98. It is safe to say that this hotel is one of the best in the city.
