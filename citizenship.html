Components of Citizenship

  • Honesty
  • Compassion
  • Respect
  • Responsibility
  • Courage
  • Activism

Citizenship

Citizenship is being a part of a community, and having
a relationship with other people in the community.


Honesty

Honesty is always being truthful to people around you. Honesty is necessary
in citizenship because a society needs honesty to maintain order and civility.

Compassion

Compassion is showing mercy and giving help to the less fortunate
Good citizens need compassion to advance other members of society.
By helping other people succeed, the society as a whole succeeds.

Respect

Respect is an admiration for somebody based on their merits. Respect is a
necessary characteristic in a good citizen, because societies have to be built
off of mutual respect to preserve civil order and a good living environent.

Responsibility

Responsibility is "doing your job", or holding yourself accountable for your actions.
A good citizen needs to be responsible doing their part in a community. If all of
the people in societies do their part, the community is able to function.

Courage

Courage is doing something scary, but ultimately right. A good citizen needs courage in order
to carry out difficult actions to help their community, such as serving in the military.

Activism

Activism is taking action in order to achieve change in society. I added this to the list
because I believe that in order for society to improve, good citizens must press for change.

One act of citizenship I perform

I am part of the Student Senate at LSRHS. The Student Senate works to solve problems
on behalf of the student body. I believe that the Student Senate is a necessary part
of our school community, as it gives students a role in school government by allowing
students to elect their peers as their representatives. As a senator, I believe that
I am performing an act of citizenship to better our school as a whole.
